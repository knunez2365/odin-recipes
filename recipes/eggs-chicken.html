Protein Meals to get swole!!

Eggs are the staple of any meal

This meal will have a good amount of proteins and carbs! Perfect thing to eat in the morning.
These are the ingredients you will need:

  • Eggs
  • Avocado Oil
  • Chicken
  • Spinach
  • Avocado

Level of difficulty: 2/10

No need to fear this is one of easiest and healthiest meals! Your brain and muscles will thank you!

Instructions:

  1. In order to make this meal easier, I would recommend buying a rotisserie chicken which should last you 3-4 days.
  2. Start by heating up the pan using Avocado oil and start making scrambled eggs, add shredded pieces of rotisserie chicken
  3. Stir until chicken and eggs are cooked! Add Avocado for healthy fats and salt and pepper accordingly
  4. Enjoy!
